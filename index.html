Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National poet of bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazri Nazrul is the national poet of Bangaladesh who is especially recognized as a bidrohi kobi around the country.Nazrul is a famous poet, musician and philosopher of Bangalesh. The famous poet Nazrul's writings deal with the revloution of that period of the british government.His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangaladesh.He has raised his voice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and got popularity swiftly around the indian subcontinent.
